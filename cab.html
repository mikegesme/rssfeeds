Alash - Free Mini Concert https://cannonab.com/events/alash-free-mini-concert/ https://cannonab.com/events/alash-free-mini-concert/ Thu, 05 Mar 2020 18:30:00 +0000 Thursday, March 5th, 12:30 pm - Cannon Falls Library Alash are masters of Tuvan throat singing, a remarkable techniques for singing multiple pitches at the same time. Masters of traditional Tuvan instruments as well as the art of throat singing, Alash are deeply committed to traditional Tuvan music and culture. Believing that traditional music must constantly evolve, the musicians subtly infuse their songs with western elements, creating their own unique style that is fresh and new, yet true to their Tuvian music heritage.
