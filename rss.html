]]> Sogn Valley Craft Fair is a Minnesota tradition; 2018 marks the 46th year bringing fine crafts, art, and local and artisinal foods to a beautiful country setting.
You’ll find a wide variety of pottery, including functional and sculptural work in raku, porcelain and stoneware, jewelry in silver, gold and beads, painting, photography, baskets, fiber weaving fabric, metalwork and more. Local music will also be featured.
The Sogn Valley Craft Fair is FREE! No admission fee and no parking fee.
